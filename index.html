Reminder Rules & Guidelines

Progress Reports: Generated at 12, 24, 26, and 48 classes attended.

Brain Tests: Conducted at 24 classes and 45 classes (last 3 classes for 48-class students).

Urgent Reminders: Marked urgent when student is within 2 classes of the threshold.

Automatic Checking: Reminders are checked automatically when attendance is recorded.

Manual Checking: Use "Check All Students" button to force reminder checking.
